Outlier Biology

Core Outlier Function

Invis

Invis is a special chemical compound only found in Phantom's body.
It's formed in shard like structures, resembles glass, is naturally sharp, acts like liquid sand, and grants invisibility.

Invis Aspects

The chemical structure of invis is volatile, and needs a structure around itself as to not dissipate into thin air. Solid and liquid material, or a separated contained space is well suited to preserve invis' structural integrity. Air and gas material easily breaks the fragile chemical structure.

Activating Invis

In order to grant invisibility, invis needs to be activated by electric energy.

In it's normal state, the invis chemical structure is compact, and does very little.
When it is activated by electricity, the chemical structure of the shards expand. The expanded form is unstable, and naturally wants to fall back to it's normal state, needing a constant source of energy to stay activated.

The amount of energy given at any time dictates how much the shards will expand. Too much will cause damage instead of benefits.

In this form, the shards become capable of guiding electromagnetic fields, such as light, right through Phantom's body - virtually making him invisible.

Dangers of Invis

Invis is not without side effects. The shard like structure and electrical sensitivity can disrupt vital- and sensory systems, becoming worse depending on the degree of activation; Too much electrical energy and the disruption can become dangerous, quickly causing damage on the micro level through tearing, corrosion, bleed, and eventually system failure.

To any normal bot, this might not seem all too dangerous, they could even utilise it themselves? There they'd be wrong. Phantom has ((Systems in place to protect him from the worst of these, which gets brought up below)), which other bots lacks.

In their case, the invis will become lethal. In concentrations around 5-10 % of their energon levels, not only will their systems get disrupted, invis shards will cut into their spark chamber, and destroy the structure of their innermost energon and spark, killing them.

The Spark

The spark controls every aspect related to the outlier. It synthesizes the invis, and triggers the invisibility.

Outlier Trigger

When the trigger function is activated, the spark converts energon into energy suitable for invis in a controlled amount. This prevents the invis from causing pain or damage to Phantom.
Although controlled, the trigger does not prevent minor side effects from invis.

Veins

The veins are an extension of the spark, and covers his whole frame. It directly connects to the spark core, and helps with the creation process of invis. Through the veins, the invis gets evenly spread into his body.

Vein Aspects

They are dark purple in natural color, (but are colored white by the invis, practically making them invisible on his body). They are soft, slightly stretchy, and grows in a veiny, tech like pattern. It may be comparable to organic matter, but does not classify as so.
The exact pattern it grows in is unique to the CNA, and always regrows in the exact same way if it were to get damaged. (This also prevents it from overgrowing.)

In the situation of an added augmentation, the veins can adapt, and spread onto anything it is in contact with over a prolonged time (Weeks/Orns)

Assistance from the Veins

The veins support the body, keeping the spark stably intact; in case of injury, keep lose plates together (if the connection is not completely severed). They also assist in fixing micro damage - which is especially helpful towards damages caused by invis.

Vital and Anti-Toxins

Unique Vital
Task

The unique vital has the ability to create antitoxins to prevent most side effects from invis. It is crucial for Phantom's well being; without it, he cannot function properly.

Location

The unique vital is located close to Phantom's spinal strut, between the thoracic and lumbar section. Thus, it is positioned directly underneath his spark and fuel pump - protected deep within his body. This position grants easy access to his main energon line.

Development

When Phantom was a sparkling, his capability of making effective antitoxins was underdeveloped. It took years for the unique vital to create a structure efficient enough to handle invis; allowing Phantom to function like a normal person.

Anti-Toxins

The antitoxins are found in Phantom's energon, and within parts of his system integrity.

Function

Antitoxins are released straight into Phantom's energon stream, and very slowly spreads into the integrity of his systems.

It gathers invis shards by letting them stab into it's structure, ending up resembling a pincushion. This prevents invis from interfering too much with systems, yet allows it to guide electromagnetic fields.

After time, antitoxins will break apart due to excessive shard concentrations. This makes constant renewal of antitoxins very important. After weeks without renewal, critical system- and sensory issues will occur.

Loss and Destruction

In case of injury or influence of destructive powers; the antitoxins can be drained from his body.
Bleeding causes the antitoxins in his energon to be spilled. Too much electrical energy will cause the invis to rapidly destroy antitoxins, in both his energon and system integrity

This makes bleeding less punishing for the antitoxin levels than too much electrical energy - as you don't lose as much antitoxins as you would getting electrocuted.
Yet both can result in a lack of antitoxins, causing problems.

Phantom only apologises when admitting to weakness, when he feels useless or unsuccessful, or just straight up being dead weight.

He's learned that his feelings of fear doesn't matter, and that he should be able to deal with everything fearlessly, or be competent enough to succeed
(Also why he hides his emotions most of the time, which in the long run damages him and puts him into vulnerable states without access to help)
This can also make him apologise for admitting to having been abused in any way. ( To him, it looks like weakness... Even tho in reality, it's an offense to him he should have never accepted. and told others about to get help )
